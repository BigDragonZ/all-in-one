# MIS 课程大纲

## 第1章：Ontological Foundations and Strategic Sourcing of Information Systems
- **核心命题**：MIS is the organizational nervous system bridging ICT and business strategy.
- **视频范围**：01-07
- **前置知识**：无
- **本章概要**：Distinguish ICT from MIS. Evaluate Make vs Buy, Outsourcing, Offshoring. Analyze SDLC methodologies (Waterfall, Agile, Open Source).

## 第2章：Distributed Architectures and Cloud-Native Infrastructures
- **核心命题**：Multi-tier architectures and cloud models optimize resource allocation and reduce CAPEX.
- **视频范围**：08-15
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Ontological_Foundations_and_Strategic_Sourcing_of_Information_Systems]]
- **本章概要**：Design N-tier Client-Server architectures. Compare LAN/WAN/Internet. Evaluate IaaS, SaaS, Private/Public/Hybrid Clouds.

## 第3章：Data Engineering - Relational Modeling and Big Data Paradigm
- **核心命题**：Structured relational models and unstructured Big Data represent two ends of the data management spectrum.
- **视频范围**：16-22
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_02_Distributed_Architectures_and_Cloud_Native_Infrastructures]]
- **本章概要**：Model relational databases with PK/FK constraints. Apply normalization and ACID properties. Use SQL for DBMS interaction. Analyze Big Data characteristics (volume, velocity, variety).

## 第4章：Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Process Orchestration
- **核心命题**：ERP systems integrate functional modules through a shared non-redundant database, creating the "top predator" of MIS.
- **视频范围**：23-31
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_03_Data_Engineering_Relational_Modeling_and_Big_Data_Paradigm]]
- **本章概要**：Map business processes (Quote-to-Cash). Analyze ERP module integration. Evaluate shared database implications. Assess vendor lock-in risks.

## 第5章：Implementation Dynamics - Socio-Technical Alignment and Governance
- **核心命题**：Successful ERP implementation requires balancing technical configuration with organizational change management.
- **视频范围**：32-39
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_04_Enterprise_Resource_Planning_ERP_and_Process_Orchestration]]
- **本章概要**：Apply BPR for As-Is to To-Be transitions. Choose between Parameterization and Code-level customization. Design Big Bang vs Modular Roll-out strategies. Manage post-implementation evolution.

## 第6章：Decision Support Systems (DSS) and Business Intelligence
- **核心命题**：DSS and BI transform raw transactional data into strategic decision-making capabilities.
- **视频范围**：40-43
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_05_Implementation_Dynamics_Socio_Technical_Alignment_and_Governance]]
- **本章概要**：Distinguish TPS vs DSS vs EIS. Classify structured vs unstructured decisions. Design Executive Dashboards with KPIs. Apply MRP and What-if simulations.

## 第7章：Big Data Analytics and Strategic Horizons
- **核心命题**：Big Data and emerging AI technologies redefine competitive advantage through pattern recognition and predictive analytics.
- **视频范围**：44-46
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_06_Decision_Support_Systems_DSS_and_Business_Intelligence]]
- **本章概要**：Process unstructured data (social media, IoT). Evaluate distributed computing frameworks (Hadoop). Analyze ML vs Deep Learning. Assess inter-organizational system integration.
