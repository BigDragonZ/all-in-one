# Operations Management 课程大纲

## 第1章：Introduction to Operations Management and Strategy
- **核心命题**：Operations management transforms inputs into value-added outputs through strategic planning, ethics, and SWOT analysis.
- **视频范围**：01-02
- **前置知识**：无
- **本章概要**：Define operations management and the transformation model. Analyze ethics, sustainability (Triple Bottom Line), and SWOT analysis for strategic decision-making.

## 第2章：Forecasting and Demand Planning
- **核心命题**：Accurate forecasting and demand planning enable effective supply chain and production scheduling.
- **视频范围**：03-05
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Introduction_to_Operations_Management_and_Strategy]]
- **本章概要**：Compare qualitative (Delphi, surveys) and quantitative (Moving Averages) forecasting methods. Analyze Peak, Seasonal, Unexpected, and Chase demand types.

## 第3章：Capacity Planning and Location Strategy
- **核心命题**：Optimal capacity utilization and strategic facility location maximize operational throughput.
- **视频范围**：06-10
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_02_Forecasting_and_Demand_Planning]]
- **本章概要**：Calculate design/effective capacity, utilization, and efficiency. Apply Lead/Lag/Match/Adjustment strategies. Use Theory of Constraints to identify bottlenecks. Evaluate location factors.

## 第4章：Process Selection and Facility Layout
- **核心命题**：Process type and facility layout must align with product volume and variety.
- **视频范围**：11-12
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_03_Capacity_Planning_and_Location_Strategy]]
- **本章概要**：Select among Continuous Flow, Assembly Line, Batch Flow, and Job Shop. Design Product-Oriented, Process-Oriented, and Fixed-Position layouts.

## 第5章：Quality Management and Standards
- **核心命题**：Quality management integrates customer expectations, cost analysis, and international standards.
- **视频范围**：13-17
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_04_Process_Selection_and_Facility_Layout]]
- **本章概要**：Analyze dimensions of quality for products and services. Evaluate Costs of Quality (failure, appraisal, prevention). Apply QFD and ISO 9000/14000 standards.

## 第6章：Continuous Improvement and Statistical Tools
- **核心命题**：Systematic improvement requires data-driven methodologies and statistical process control.
- **视频范围**：18-22
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_05_Quality_Management_and_Standards]]
- **本章概要**：Apply Six Sigma (DMAIC/DMADV) and PDCA cycles. Utilize 7 SPC tools. Conduct Value Stream Mapping and Benchmarking.

## 第7章：Inventory Management Systems
- **核心命题**：Inventory optimization balances holding costs against stockout risks through quantitative models.
- **视频范围**：23-25
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_06_Continuous_Improvement_and_Statistical_Tools]]
- **本章概要**：Classify inventory types (raw, WIP, finished, safety). Compare Periodic vs Perpetual systems. Apply EOQ, EPQ, and ABC Analysis.

## 第8章：Operational Planning, Scheduling, and Lean Systems
- **核心命题**：Lean systems and hierarchical planning synchronize production with actual customer demand.
- **视频范围**：26-32
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_07_Inventory_Management_Systems]]
- **本章概要**：Differentiate Aggregate Planning, MPS, MRP/MRP II/ERP. Implement JIT, eliminate 7 Wastes, and design Kanban systems. Apply service and product scheduling strategies.

## 第9章：Supply Chain, Logistics, and Operational Economics
- **核心命题**：Strategic supply chain design and cost analysis drive competitive advantage and profitability.
- **视频范围**：33-40
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_08_Operational_Planning_Scheduling_and_Lean_Systems]]
- **本章概要**：Select Lean vs Agile supply chains. Evaluate Vertical Integration and Strategic Alliances. Manage logistics (inbound, outbound, global, 3PL, reverse). Calculate breakeven points and economies of scale.
