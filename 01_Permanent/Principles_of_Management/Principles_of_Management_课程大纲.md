# Principles of Management 课程大纲

## 第1章：Introduction to Management
- **核心命题**：Management is the art of getting things done through people, balancing objectives, resources, and organizational effectiveness.
- **视频范围**：01-06
- **前置知识**：无
- **本章概要**：Define management and its objectives. Understand levels of management (top, middle, lower). Compare leadership vs management. Explore functional areas and core functions (POLC: Planning, Organizing, Leading, Controlling).

## 第2章：Classical Management Theories
- **核心命题**：Classical theories provide foundational frameworks that still influence modern organizational design.
- **视频范围**：07-08
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Introduction_to_Management]]
- **本章概要**：Analyze Henry Fayol's 14 Principles of Management. Evaluate F.W. Taylor's Scientific Management theory. Compare contributions and limitations of classical approaches.

## 第3章：Planning
- **核心命题**：Planning is the primary management function that establishes objectives and determines the course of action to achieve them.
- **视频范围**：10-14
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_02_Classical_Management_Theories]]
- **本章概要**：Understand planning approaches, advantages, and disadvantages. Master the planning process and principles. Classify types of planning (strategic, tactical, operational). Apply budget planning techniques.

## 第4章：Management by Objectives (MBO)
- **核心命题**：MBO aligns individual and organizational goals through participative objective-setting and performance measurement.
- **视频范围**：15-17
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_03_Planning]]
- **本章概要**：Understand MBO introduction and process. Classify MBO types. Evaluate benefits, weaknesses, and guidelines for setting effective objectives.

## 第5章：Organizing
- **核心命题**：Organizing creates the structural framework that allocates resources and coordinates activities to achieve organizational goals.
- **视频范围**：18-25
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_04_Management_by_Objectives_MBO]]
- **本章概要**：Understand organizing principles. Compare formal vs informal organizations. Analyze functional and committee organizational structures. Evaluate span of management and delegation of authority.
