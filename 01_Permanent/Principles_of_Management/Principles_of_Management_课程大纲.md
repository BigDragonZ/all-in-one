# Principles of Management 课程大纲

## 第1章：Introduction to the Fundamentals of Management
- **核心命题**：Management is the art of getting things done through people, balancing the 4 M's (Men, Material, Money, Machines).
- **视频范围**：01-06
- **前置知识**：无
- **本章概要**：Define management and its nature. Understand objectives and importance. Explore levels of management, qualities of a manager, and leadership vs management.

## 第2章：Management Perspectives - Fayol and Taylor
- **核心命题**：Classical theories provide foundational frameworks that still influence modern organizational design.
- **视频范围**：07-08
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Introduction_to_the_Fundamentals_of_Management]]
- **本章概要**：Analyze Henry Fayol's 14 Principles of Management. Evaluate F.W. Taylor's Scientific Management theory. Compare contributions and limitations of classical approaches.

## 第3章：The Managerial Profile - Roles, Qualities, and Levels
- **核心命题**：Effective managers require distinct qualities and operate at different hierarchical levels with specific responsibilities.
- **视频范围**：03-04
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Introduction_to_the_Fundamentals_of_Management]]
- **本章概要**：Identify levels of management (Top, Middle, Lower). Evaluate qualities of a manager. Distinguish leadership from management.

## 第4章：Functional Areas and Core Management Functions
- **核心命题**：Management functions (POSDCC) and functional areas provide the operational framework for organizational success.
- **视频范围**：05-06
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Introduction_to_the_Fundamentals_of_Management]]
- **本章概要**：Understand POSDCC framework (Planning, Organizing, Staffing, Directing, Coordinating, Controlling). Explore five functional areas: Production, Marketing, Finance, Personnel, Office Management.

## 第5章：Planning - Strategy and Decision Making
- **核心命题**：Planning is the primary management function that establishes objectives and determines the course of action to achieve them.
- **视频范围**：10-14
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_04_Functional_Areas_and_Core_Management_Functions]]
- **本章概要**：Master the 8-step planning process. Evaluate planning approaches (Top-down, Bottom-up, Composite, Team). Classify types of planning (Strategic, Tactical, Operational, Standing, Single-use). Apply budget planning techniques.

## 第6章：Management by Objectives (MBO) and Budgetary Planning
- **核心命题**：MBO aligns individual and organizational goals through participative objective-setting and performance measurement.
- **视频范围**：15-17
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_05_Planning_Strategy_and_Decision_Making]]
- **本章概要**：Understand MBO philosophy and process. Classify objectives (Primary, Secondary, Individual, Social). Evaluate benefits, weaknesses, and guidelines for setting effective objectives. Apply budget planning as a control tool.

## 第7章：Organizing - Structure and Design
- **核心命题**：Organizing creates the structural framework that allocates resources and coordinates activities to achieve organizational goals.
- **视频范围**：18-20
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_06_Management_by_Objectives_MBO_and_Budgetary_Planning]]
- **本章概要**：Understand organizing fundamentals and principles. Compare formal vs informal organizations. Analyze the four-step organizing process.

## 第8章：Functional and Committee Forms of Organization
- **核心命题**：Different organizational structures optimize expertise, coordination, and decision-making efficiency.
- **视频范围**：22-23
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_07_Organizing_Structure_and_Design]]
- **本章概要**：Analyze functional organization (expert-led departments). Evaluate committee organization (collective thinking). Compare pros and cons of specialized structures.

## 第9章：The Dynamics of Supervision - Span of Management
- **核心命题**：The span of management determines organizational structure (flat vs tall) and supervision effectiveness.
- **视频范围**：24
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_08_Functional_and_Committee_Forms_of_Organization]]
- **本章概要**：Define span of control and its structural impact. Analyze direct, group, and cross relationships. Evaluate 11 factors influencing span of management.

## 第10章：Empowerment through Delegation of Authority
- **核心命题**：Effective delegation requires balancing authority, responsibility, and accountability while maintaining unity of command.
- **视频范围**：25
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_09_The_Dynamics_of_Supervision_Span_of_Management]]
- **本章概要**：Understand the authority-responsibility link. Apply seven principles of delegation. Ensure decision-level parity and scalar chain integrity.
