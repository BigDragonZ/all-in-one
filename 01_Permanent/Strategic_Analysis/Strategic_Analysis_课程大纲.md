# Strategic Analysis 课程大纲

## 第1章：Foundations of Strategic Management
- **核心命题**：Strategic management is the process of analysis, formulation, and implementation that ensures long-term business success through direction, focus, and resource alignment.
- **视频范围**：01
- **前置知识**：无
- **本章概要**：Define strategic management and its key stages. Understand the importance of direction, focus, and resource alignment. Analyze how strategic management helps organizations anticipate challenges and seize opportunities.

## 第2章：The Three Levels of Strategy
- **核心命题**：Strategy operates at corporate, business, and functional levels, requiring coherence and consistency across all tiers for organizational success.
- **视频范围**：03
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_01_Foundations_of_Strategic_Management]]
- **本章概要**：Differentiate corporate strategy (overall direction, portfolio management), business strategy (competitive advantage, cost leadership, differentiation), and functional strategy (departmental policies in marketing, finance, HR).

## 第3章：External Environment Analysis
- **核心命题**：Organizations must systematically evaluate external forces to identify opportunities and threats in their competitive landscape.
- **视频范围**：04
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_02_The_Three_Levels_of_Strategy]]
- **本章概要**：Apply PESTLE framework (Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, Legal). Evaluate industry dynamics using Porter's Five Forces (threat of entrants, supplier/buyer power, substitutes, competitive rivalry).

## 第4章：Internal Environment Analysis
- **核心命题**：Sustainable competitive advantage derives from unique internal resources, capabilities, and core competencies that are difficult to imitate.
- **视频范围**：05
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_03_External_Environment_Analysis]]
- **本章概要**：Identify and categorize tangible and intangible resources. Distinguish capabilities from core competencies. Analyze organizational culture, leadership, and operational processes as strategic assets.

## 第5章：Integrating Analysis with SWOT
- **核心命题**：SWOT synthesis transforms discrete internal and external analyses into actionable strategic priorities.
- **视频范围**：06
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_04_Internal_Environment_Analysis]]
- **本章概要**：Conduct structured SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats). Develop strategies leveraging strengths to seize opportunities while mitigating threats and weaknesses. Apply SWOT to resource allocation and risk management.

## 第6章：The Four Pillars of Strategic Management
- **核心命题**：Effective strategic management rests on four pillars: Analysis, Formulation, Implementation, and Evaluation, forming a continuous cycle.
- **视频范围**：02
- **前置知识**：[[Ch_05_Integrating_Analysis_with_SWOT]]
- **本章概要**：Understand the four pillars as an integrated framework. Analyze how environmental scanning informs strategy formulation. Evaluate implementation challenges and the importance of continuous feedback and adaptation.
