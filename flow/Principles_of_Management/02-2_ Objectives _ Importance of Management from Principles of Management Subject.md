### 管理的目标 (Objectives of Management)

The main objective of any organization is earning profit and ensuring the smooth running of the organization. If these two are going well, the organization will be successful because of the management's functions and objectives. They are managing everything. So, in-depth, we'll see what the objectives are, apart from profit maximization and the smooth running of the organization. There are four main objectives.

1.  **资源的合理利用 (Proper Utilization of Resources)**
    A company has a lot of resources, and the utilization of these resources is the main objective of any management. If we can utilize these resources properly, there is no doubt that the organization is heading towards progress. What kind of resources? The first resource is **Men**, the people who are working in the organization. Then, **Material**, which is used for production. Next is **Money**; where are we getting the money, and are we utilizing it properly? And finally, **Machines**. These four M's—**Men, Material, Money, and Machines**—if you can handle them excellently, that's great.
    - **Men**: How many employees are there? Are they working properly? Do their skills need to be upgraded with new technology?
    - **Material**: Which material are we buying? What is the cost? How can we minimize the material cost and get quality material?
    - **Money**: How much money do we require? From where are we getting the money? Are we utilizing it properly?
    - **Machines**: This includes equipment and furniture. How much is required? Are we buying it, utilizing it properly, or wasting it? Is there any overspending?
    All these things come under the proper utilization of resources.

2.  **提升绩效 (Improving Performance)**
    This means improving the performance of the employees and the total organization. How can we improve the organization's performance? By earning profit. How do we earn profit? By selling more goods. How can we sell more goods? When we keep the price low. And how can we keep the price low? When the cost is less. If the cost is less, we can sell at a proper rate. If we can sell at a proper rate, we get more sales. When we get more sales, we get more profit. It's all interlinked.
    To improve performance, it's better to segregate performance department-wise, such as the sales department, purchase department, finance department, and HR department. If you have control department-wise and the middle management is good, automatically the performance of the organization will be good. To get good performance, what is needed is proper planning by the management. If they have proper planning, then automatically half of the work is done. The rest is just to implement it.

3.  **调动最优秀的人才 (Mobilizing Best Talent)**
    This refers to mobilizing the talent of employees. There are some employees in the organization whose talent is very high; they have very good technological and organizing skills. If you just motivate them, give them some perks, or any kind of extra benefit (fringe benefits), and recognize them, it will motivate those employees. That is why you need to focus on the mobilization of the best talent. Identify the talent of the employees, extract it, motivate them, and give them some benefits so that they'll be very happy to work with the organization.

4.  **规划未来 (Planning for Future)**
    Without planning, there is no work at all. If you do proper planning, the future will be good. We just need to implement it once the planning is over. These are the main objectives: proper utilization of resources, improving performance, mobilizing the best talent, and planning for the future.

### 管理的重要性 (Importance of Management)

Now we'll see the significance or importance of management. This is also very important to get a command over the management subject. You need to have a strong base and understand the basics of management.

1.  **实现团队目标 (Achievement of Group Objectives)**
    The management handles group objectives. To align all group activities and objectives with the organization's objectives, management is the only entity that can handle this. Give each group some target, handle them, and keep an eye on their performance. If any changes are required, continuously focus and take different steps. Automatically, group objectives will become organization objectives.

2.  **资源的最佳利用 (Optimum Utilization of Resources)**
    As we discussed in the objectives, the utilization of resources is also a key importance of management. The resources are **Land, Labor, Capital, and Organization**.
    - **Land**: Whether we buy it or take it on lease, is its utilization proper?
    - **Labor**: The employees.
    - **Capital**: The money.
    - You can also use the "4 M's" here: **Men, Material, Money, and Machines**. The optimum utilization of these resources is one of the significant aspects of any management.

3.  **成本最小化 (Minimization of Cost)**
    The cost, specifically the product cost, should always be less. If the cost is less, consumers are willing to buy the product. You can attract all kinds of consumers: high class, middle class, and lower class. How can we decrease the cost? It mainly depends on the cost of material, labor cost, and overheads. If we can control these three, the cost will come down. When the cost is down, sales will be more.

4.  **增加利润 (Increased Profits)**
    We have to increase profits. If there are no profits, there is no organization. Simply earning the same profit every year makes it difficult to retain employees and survive. The ambition and focus should always be to increase the profit. For this, once again, planning is key. For example, if last year's profit was 20 crores, and this year we expect 30 or 40 crores, what is your strategy to achieve that?

5.  **业务的平稳运行 (Smooth Running of Business)**
    The organization has to run smoothly, without any disturbances. These disturbances could be financial (capital is not sufficient), related to employees (scarcity of skilled employees), or from external competition (new technology used by others). We need to be very careful from every angle to ensure the smooth running of the organization.

6.  **提供创新与变革 (Provides Innovation and Change)**
    Technology is changing, and new methodologies are coming. If we stick to old-fashioned technology, we are going to lose consumers. They will switch to another product. That is why management must always focus on innovative new ideas and adapt to growth and change. We must follow market changes, customer requirements, and changes in consumer tastes and fashion. Old methodology is not going to work in this present cutthroat competitive world.

7.  **履行社会义务 (Meeting Social Obligations)**
    An organization has social obligations. This includes providing products at a cheap rate, offering quality products, creating employment, and paying taxes to the government. In this way, the management is also fulfilling its social responsibilities.

8.  **在发展中经济体（如印度）中的特殊重要性 (Special Importance in a Developing Economy like India's)**
    From an industry point of view, India is a developing country, moving towards becoming a developed country. This is because of its industries. If management is proper in every organization, they will earn a profit. When they get profit, provide good employment, pay taxes, and develop the standard of living of the people, the growth will be quite fast. That is why India's economic development is dependent on management.
