## 精修内容

This is the last and brief video in this MOOC after six intensive units or weeks. When we started, we said that we would try to provide an overall introduction to what Management Information Systems are, how they shape day-to-day business life and our own current or future job, the supporting infrastructures such as the cloud or big data, and the main integrated systems—the ERPs, such as SAP or the open-source alternatives.

We hope that we have met these initial objectives and that you have found this course helpful and a productive investment of your time. We are all well aware of how challenging the course was, even though we tried to adopt a light touch and a managerial, rather than a purely technological, approach. We hope that we found an acceptable balance between clarity and conceptual soundness.

We also hope that the self-paced format has provided enough flexibility to reconcile the workload involved in this course with your other commitments.

And now, it is time for a "see you again soon." Farewell. It was a real pleasure sharing this course with all of you. On behalf of the whole course team, all the best and see you soon.
