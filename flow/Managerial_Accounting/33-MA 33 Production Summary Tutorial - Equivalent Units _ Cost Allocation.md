Let's do a production summary. The overall goal of a production summary is to take the actual units and convert them to **equivalent units**.
- **Section one** is the manufacturing cost. We're just going to add it up and call it "manufacturing cost to be allocated."
- **Section two** is our equivalent units, which answers "How much work did we do?"
- **Section three** combines section one (cost) and section two (units). We take the cost divided by the units to get the unit cost based on equivalent units.
- **Section four** is where we multiply everything back out to get our manufacturing cost that we allocate to **cost of goods manufactured** (the finished goods) and to the ending work in process.
